Contact

Planning your special day? I would love to hear from you! Getting in touch with me is super simple, just pop me over an email over with some super important details.

What I need to know:

  • Your name
  • Preferred contact information (phone/email)
  • Wedding date & location
  • Type of enquiry (Just incase its not a wedding!)
  • A little message letting me know anything else you think is important!

So what happens next?
I love meeting up with prospective couples to discuss everything you want from your day, let you know what I can offer & hear all about your plans!

A little too far away?
Not a problem! I am based in South Wales and am happy to travel to you but if you’re a little too far away phone calls and Skype can work wonders!

I aim to respond to all enquiries within 48 hours (but check your junk folder just incase!) I’m so excited to hear from you!

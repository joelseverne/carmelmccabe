All You Need is Love!

Hello, howdy and hi!

My name is Carmel Teresa McCabe and I am quite frankly OBSESSED with weddings and everything about them!

For several years now I have been working in various fields of photography, both commercially and personally. Since the planning of my own wedding my love of love knows no bounds! I truly feel so privileged and honored to share in the joy of so many peoples wedding day. Combining my passion of creating beautiful imagery celebrating love!

My own wedding journey started when I made the move from the land locked village where I grew up down to South wales to study photography. During this time I have met some of the most fantastic people, the Welsh really do know how to offer a warm welcome! One of these people was my now husband, Killian. And after a whirlwind start, 7 years later we are happily married!

Our engagement was looooong! Close to 3 years in fact! And in those years I fully submerged myself in the joys of wedding planning. It was during this time that I realised that surely there is nothing better in life than celebrating love?! You dare you to try and convince me otherwise! My wedding day was truly the happiest and most perfect day of my life and my job allows me to share that beautiful feeling with other people over and over again!

Nothing could be better!

My first wedding - Sam & Matt

August marked the 5 year wedding anniversary of Sam & Matt Dabb. Friends of mine that entrusted me with the photography of their wedding.

To say this was my first wedding is a little bit of a pork pie as I used to work as second photographer back in my late teens. This was however my first official orchestrated gig, from bridal prep to first dance!

I had know Sam and Matt for awhile before the wedding having worked with them both in my favourite pub and music venue Le Pub in Newport. And their wedding was beautiful! So relaxed and colourful (a photographers dream really) The weather was perfect and the guests were up for anything. More weddings like this please!!

A Surprise Wedding! - Tahnee and Paul

Early September brought a beautiful, fun and quite frankly super surprise wedding at the stunning venue of The Globe is Hay on Wye. Why was the wedding surprising I hear you ask? Well, I will tell you!

After a hazy week away visiting my parents for my birthday during the drive home I had a phone call from a previous client of mine needing an emergency photographer for a wedding for the very next day! A grand total of 15 hours passed and I was at the venue photographing one of the cutest countryside weddings ever!

The bride, Tahnee is another friend of mine. An absolutely beautiful soul who picked a dress to match by e&w couture, one of my favourite local designers. Whilst her sparkly new husband Paul, who is a celebrity in the Haiku writing world made the whole room weep during his speech.

The whole day was so filled with love with a smiley, happy ceremony, a gorgeous venue and dreamy weather. Having never been to Hay On wye before I was eager to explore the village with my couple. We wandered around the cute higgledy piggledy streets and as both the bride and groom are massive literary lovers we picked one of their favourite book shops for a couple of portraits.

I was so happy that I could help out on the day and help elevate any wedding day nightmares from happening. Thank you so much guys, I loved every minute!
